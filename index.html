About the App

Green Trace is an app that helps you track your CO2 emissions based on your daily purchases, allowing you to make more sustainable choices.

Key Features:

  • Simple CO2 tracking based on transaction data
  • Personalized carbon footprint insights
  • Integrates with your bank to get real-time transaction data
  • Easy-to-understand dashboard
  • Set goals to reduce your carbon emissions

How It Works

  1. Sign up and link your bank account
  2. Track your transactions automatically
  3. View your carbon footprint and take actions to reduce it
  4. Set goals and monitor your progress over time
